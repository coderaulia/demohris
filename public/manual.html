HR Performance Suite Manual

Quick guide for daily use by Employee, Manager, HR, and Super Admin.

Panduan HR Performance Suite

Panduan singkat penggunaan harian untuk Employee, Manager, HR, dan Super Admin.

1. Login & Account

  • Enter your registered email and password, then select Sign In.
  • Use Forgot Password on the login page to request reset link.
  • After login, use Password in the top-right header to change your password.

2. Navigation Tabs

  • Dashboard: Summary, KPI analytics, leadership trend, and risk overview.
  • Employees: Employee master data (Super Admin).
  • Assessment & KPI: Competency assessment and KPI input workflow.
  • Records: Assessment Records, KPI Records, and Probation & PIP.
  • Settings: Users, competencies, KPI definition, KPI monthly target config, and org setup.

3. Common Workflow

  1. Check KPI summary, leadership analytics, and updates in Dashboard.
  2. Create or review assessments in Assessment & KPI.
  3. Input KPI achievements and review KPI period values in Assessment & KPI.
  4. Validate final records and exports in Records.
  5. Maintain users/configuration in Settings (if authorized).

4. Assessment Flow

  1. Open Assessment & KPI.
  2. From Select Employee, choose employee to assess.
  3. Click Assess Competencies.
  4. Select position and fill competency scores.
  5. Click Review Answers, then Final Submit.
  6. Verify result in Records > Assessment Records.

5. KPI Record Flow (Input, Edit, Validation)

  1. Open Assessment & KPI, select employee, click Input KPI Achievement.
  2. Set Period (YYYY-MM), select KPI metric, and input value.
  3. KPI metric list follows active definition by effective month and your role scope.
  4. If KPI unit is %, fill Attained and Total to auto-calculate final value.
  5. Click Submit Achievement.
  6. System stores KPI snapshots (target/name/unit/category) at record time, so future KPI edits do not change old records.
  7. Open Records > KPI Records to validate saved data.
  8. Use row actions in KPI Records to Edit or Delete wrong entry (according to role scope).

6. KPI Definition, Governance, Monthly Target Config & Probation Rules

  1. Open Settings > KPI Management.
  2. To add/edit KPI definition, fill KPI name, category/position, target, unit, effective month, and optional change note.
  3. Use KPI Governance toggle to require HR approval for manager KPI changes.
  4. If governance is enabled, changes appear in Pending KPI Approvals until approved by HR/Super Admin.
  5. All submitted changes are recorded in KPI Version History (definition and target versions).
  6. Manager can add/edit KPI definition for team positions only; Super Admin can manage all.
  7. In Employee Targets Config, select target month and employee.
  8. Input monthly target override per KPI, then click Save Employee Targets.
  9. Leave fields blank to fall back to global/default KPI target.
  10. Open Settings > General to configure probation scoring policy (Work/Managing/Attitude weights, pass threshold, attendance deduction JSON rules).
  11. Weight values are normalized by system to keep total score policy-safe (100).

7. Probation Review & PIP Flow

  1. Open Records > Probation & PIP.
  2. Click Generate Probation Drafts for employees reaching probation end.
  3. Use Review Form to fill monthly manager inputs: managing task score and qualitative text.
  4. Work Performance score is calculated from KPI records by probation date window.
  5. Attendance / Attitude score is managed via Add Attendance Entry (HR/Super Admin), using deduction suggestions from configured attendance rules, then auto-recomputed.
  6. Set decision: Pending, Pass, Extend, or Fail.
  7. Click Export Probation Excel to download monthly sheets (Bulan 1-3 + Rekap).
  8. For low KPI performance, click Generate PIP and track status in PIP Plans.
Probation windows are date-to-date from join date. Example: join on 2025-12-25 means month windows are 2025-12-25 to 2026-01-24, 2026-01-25 to 2026-02-24, and 2026-02-25 to 2026-03-24.

8. Report Filters

  • Use top filters (Department, Manager, Period) to scope Dashboard and Records outputs.
  • Select Clear to reset all filters.

9. Exports & Imports

  • Export options are available in relevant modules (CSV, JSON, Excel, PDF).
  • Before importing files, verify format and required columns/fields.

10. Session & Security

  • Inactive sessions are logged out automatically after timeout.
  • Always log out from shared devices.

11. Troubleshooting

  • Data not visible: confirm your role and selected filters.
  • KPI change not applied yet: check Pending KPI Approvals and approval status.
  • Cannot save/update: check required fields and network connection.
  • Error email rate limit exceeded: too many auth emails in short time, wait and retry.
  • Access issue persists: contact your system administrator.

1. Login & Akun

  • Masukkan email dan password yang sudah terdaftar, lalu klik Sign In.
  • Gunakan Forgot Password di halaman login untuk meminta link reset password.
  • Setelah login, gunakan menu Password di header kanan atas untuk mengganti password.

2. Tab Navigasi

  • Dashboard: Ringkasan, analitik KPI, tren leadership, dan overview risiko.
  • Employees: Data master karyawan (Super Admin).
  • Assessment & KPI: Alur input assessment kompetensi dan KPI.
  • Records: Assessment Records, KPI Records, dan Probation & PIP.
  • Settings: Users, kompetensi, definisi KPI, konfigurasi target KPI bulanan, dan organisasi.

3. Workflow Umum

  1. Cek ringkasan KPI, analitik leadership, dan update terbaru di Dashboard.
  2. Buat atau review assessment di Assessment & KPI.
  3. Input pencapaian KPI dan review nilai KPI per periode di Assessment & KPI.
  4. Validasi record final dan export di Records.
  5. Kelola user dan konfigurasi di Settings (jika memiliki akses).

4. Alur Assessment

  1. Buka Assessment & KPI.
  2. Pada Select Employee, pilih karyawan yang akan dinilai.
  3. Klik Assess Competencies.
  4. Pilih posisi dan isi skor kompetensi.
  5. Klik Review Answers, lalu Final Submit.
  6. Verifikasi hasil di Records > Assessment Records.

5. Alur KPI Record (Input, Edit, Validasi)

  1. Buka Assessment & KPI, pilih karyawan, lalu klik Input KPI Achievement.
  2. Tentukan Period (YYYY-MM), pilih metric KPI, lalu isi nilainya.
  3. Daftar metric KPI mengikuti definisi aktif berdasarkan effective month dan scope role Anda.
  4. Jika unit KPI adalah %, isi Attained dan Total agar sistem menghitung nilai akhir otomatis.
  5. Klik Submit Achievement.
  6. Sistem menyimpan snapshot KPI (target/nama/unit/kategori) saat record dibuat, jadi perubahan KPI di masa depan tidak mengubah record lama.
  7. Buka Records > KPI Records untuk validasi data yang tersimpan.
  8. Gunakan aksi pada baris KPI Records untuk Edit atau Delete entri yang salah (sesuai scope role).

6. KPI Definition, Governance, Monthly Target Config & Aturan Probation

  1. Buka Settings > KPI Management.
  2. Untuk menambah atau mengubah definisi KPI, isi nama KPI, kategori/posisi, target, unit, effective month, dan catatan perubahan jika perlu.
  3. Gunakan toggle KPI Governance untuk mewajibkan approval HR pada perubahan KPI oleh manager.
  4. Jika governance aktif, perubahan akan masuk ke Pending KPI Approvals sampai disetujui oleh HR atau Super Admin.
  5. Semua perubahan tersimpan di KPI Version History (versi definisi dan versi target).
  6. Manager hanya bisa menambah atau mengubah KPI untuk posisi di timnya; Super Admin bisa mengelola semua KPI.
  7. Di Employee Targets Config, pilih bulan target dan karyawan.
  8. Isi override target bulanan per KPI, lalu klik Save Employee Targets.
  9. Biarkan field kosong jika ingin mengikuti target global/default KPI.
  10. Buka Settings > General untuk mengatur kebijakan scoring probation (bobot Work/Managing/Attitude, pass threshold, dan aturan potongan attendance dalam format JSON).
  11. Bobot akan dinormalisasi otomatis oleh sistem agar total kebijakan tetap aman (100).

7. Alur Probation Review & PIP

  1. Buka Records > Probation & PIP.
  2. Klik Generate Probation Drafts untuk karyawan yang sudah mendekati akhir masa probation.
  3. Gunakan Review Form untuk mengisi input bulanan dari manager: managing task score dan qualitative text.
  4. Skor Work Performance dihitung dari KPI records berdasarkan jendela tanggal probation.
  5. Skor Attendance / Attitude dikelola melalui Add Attendance Entry (HR/Super Admin), memakai saran deduction dari aturan attendance yang sudah dikonfigurasi, lalu dihitung ulang otomatis.
  6. Tentukan decision: Pending, Pass, Extend, atau Fail.
  7. Klik Export Probation Excel untuk mengunduh sheet bulanan (Bulan 1-3 + Rekap).
  8. Untuk performa KPI yang rendah, klik Generate PIP dan pantau statusnya di PIP Plans.
Jendela probation dihitung date-to-date dari join date. Contoh: join pada 2025-12-25 berarti jendela bulan 1 adalah 2025-12-25 sampai 2026-01-24, bulan 2 adalah 2026-01-25 sampai 2026-02-24, dan bulan 3 adalah 2026-02-25 sampai 2026-03-24.

8. Report Filters

  • Gunakan filter atas (Department, Manager, Period) untuk membatasi output Dashboard dan Records.
  • Pilih Clear untuk mereset semua filter.

9. Export & Import

  • Opsi export tersedia di modul yang relevan (CSV, JSON, Excel, PDF).
  • Sebelum import file, pastikan format dan kolom atau field wajib sudah sesuai.

10. Session & Security

  • Session yang tidak aktif akan logout otomatis setelah timeout.
  • Selalu logout jika menggunakan perangkat bersama.

11. Troubleshooting

  • Data tidak terlihat: pastikan role dan filter yang dipilih sudah benar.
  • Perubahan KPI belum aktif: cek Pending KPI Approvals dan status approval.
  • Tidak bisa save atau update: cek field wajib dan koneksi jaringan.
  • Error email rate limit exceeded: terlalu banyak email auth dalam waktu singkat, tunggu lalu coba lagi.
  • Jika masalah akses masih berlanjut: hubungi system administrator Anda.
